# Org‑Wide Governance Visibility

## Purpose
Provide **observability**, not enforcement,
across all governed repositories.

---

## Signals

- Repo health
- Drift presence
- Change classification
- Phase‑2 compliance

---

## Outputs

- Aggregated org snapshots
- Dashboards
- Reports
- Heatmaps

---

## Invariants

- Visibility is read‑only.
- No auto‑remediation.
- No enforcement authority.
- No mutation of repos.

---

## Consumers

- Governance teams
- Security
- Leadership
- Auditors
