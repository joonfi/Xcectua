# Governance SLOs

## Purpose
Define **expectations**, not gates,
for governance execution.

---

## Examples

- 95% of validations complete within N seconds
- TYPE‑3 changes cost more by design

---

## Rules

- SLOs are advisory.
- Breaches do not block merges.
- Trends matter more than absolutes.

---

## Anti‑Patterns

- Optimizing away validation
- Treating SLOs as enforcement
- Bypassing governance for speed
