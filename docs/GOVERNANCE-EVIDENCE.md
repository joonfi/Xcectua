# Governance Evidence

## Principle
> If it isn’t evidenced and validated, it isn’t released.

---

## Evidence Rules

- Evidence is explicit.
- Evidence is local‑first.
- Evidence is never auto‑published.
- Evidence does not validate itself.

---

## Evidence Types

- Diff outputs
- Rationale documents
- Test traces
- Doctor snapshots
- Analysis notes

---

## Relationship to Validation

Evidence supports governance review.
Only `rexce` validates correctness.

---

## Automation

Phase‑3 tooling may:
- check completeness
- bundle evidence
- surface gaps

Phase‑3 tooling may **not**:
- judge sufficiency
- approve changes
